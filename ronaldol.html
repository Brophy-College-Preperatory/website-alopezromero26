Cristiano Ronaldo: The G.O.A.T.

Ronaldo was born on February 5, 1985, and he is a Portuguese forward.

Starting at Sporting CP, he rose to fame and the world began to see his talent and hard work as he moved to Manchester United.

In 2009, he transferred to Real Madrid and has been a Real Madrid legend since. He carried his team to four Champions League trophies in five years.

Now he is starring at Al Nassr, aiming for 1000 goals while also becoming a global internet sensation.
